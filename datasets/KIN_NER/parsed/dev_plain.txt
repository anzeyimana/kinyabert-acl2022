James & Daniella
James Rugarama na Daniella Mukiza ni imwe muri Couple zimaze gushinga imizi mu muziki wo kuramya Imana .
Yamamaye mu ndirimbo zirimo ' Mpa Amavuta ' , ' Nkoresha ' n ' izindi .
Baherutse no kumurika album yabo ya mbere .
Iyi couple ifitanye abana babiri , ni imwe mu yakoze umuziki ukabasha kugera ku kigero nk' icyo uyu munsi bariho .
Bavuga ko kuba indirimbo zabo zamamaye cyane mu buryo bwihuse ari ubushake bw' Imana ndetse n' amasengesho yabo adasiba .
Daniella avuga ko urukundo rwabo rwatangiye gututumba mu 2008 na 2009 , mu 2012 bajya kwiga muri Uganda , umugabo yiga iby' ububanyi n' amahanga mu gihe umugore icyo gihe yigaga ibaruramari .
Mu 2015 nibwo barushinze .
James avuga ko ajya guhitamo Daniella ikintu yamukundiye ari uko yari umukobwa w' umutima kandi ukunda Imana cyane .
Ati " Twari inshuti cyane ntabwo navuga ko namukundiye iki ariko yari umukobwa ukunda Imana , kandi burya hari n' ukuntu uhuza n' umuntu .
Natwe twarahuye biba mahwi .
Byahereye cyane ko yakundaga Imana ari umuririmbyi mwiza ugasanga duhuza ibintu byose .
Daniella we yemeza ko umugabo ari umuntu w' inyangamugayo mu buryo bukomeye ku buryo ari ikintu ashimira Imana , ikindi akaba ari umuntu uvuga biri ngombwa .
Yemeza ko kumusaba ko bakundana bitamugoye cyane ko akenshi babaga bari kumwe cyane ndetse babitsanya amabanga .
Ubu bafite abana babiri umuhungu n' umukobwa .
Kuba ari couple ikora umurimo w' Imana wo kuririmba , bavuga ko ari ikintu kibafasha kuko kuba bafite icyerekezo kimwe ari ikintu gihambaye ama - couple yose yakabaye yitaho cyane .
Daniella we avuga ko umuryango ukorera Imana hamwe ntaho uba uhuriye na wundi usanga buri umwe aba muri ibi undi mu bindi .
